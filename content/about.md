John Vincent Miller is a business development and product leader with more than 15 years of experience driving growth, market expansion, and customer adoption across the aviation and enterprise technology sectors. His career spans flight planning, digital cockpit, and aviation operations software, with a track record that includes multi-million-dollar B2B deals, global product deployments, and go-to-market strategy for some of the most recognized names in aviation technology.

Vincent currently serves as Product Manager for International Expansion and NOTAMs at ForeFlight (now Jeppesen ForeFlight), where he owns the roadmap for region-specific functionality that closes competitive gaps for a global user base, and where he established the company's first dedicated NOTAM product function. Earlier in his career, he led bid strategy for multi-million-dollar aviation technology contracts as Bid Manager at SITA FOR AIRCRAFT, ran global delivery and deployment operations as Head of Delivery, Services and Deployment at NAVBLUE, and generated more than $3 million in B2B revenue as Senior Product Consultant for Lido Navigation at Lufthansa Systems — including securing the company's first South American customer and expanding its commercial presence across the Americas and APAC.

Vincent began his aviation career in business development at FlightAware, supporting enterprise aviation data services, and built broader consulting and strategy experience as a practice lead at Synechron and in business development roles spanning the defense and energy sectors. He holds an MBA with concentrations in Strategy and Finance from SDA Bocconi School of Management, including an exchange term at The Wharton School, and a BBA in International Business and Finance from The George Washington University.

A licensed private pilot with an instrument rating, Vincent brings a practitioner's perspective to the technology shaping how aircraft are planned, dispatched, and flown — equally comfortable in the boardroom negotiating a deal and in the cockpit experiencing firsthand what these tools are built to support.

This blog is where he tracks and comments on the deals, products, and AI developments reshaping flight operations technology.
