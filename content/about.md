Vincent Miller is an aviation leader with more than 15 years of experience driving growth, market expansion, and customer adoption across the aviation and flight ops technology sectors. His career spans flight planning, digital cockpit, and aviation operations software, with a track record that includes multi-million-dollar B2B deals, global product deployments, and go-to-market strategy for some of the most recognized names in aviation technology.

Vincent has served as Product Manager Jeppesen ForeFlight, as Bid Manager at SITA for AIRCRAFT, as Head of Delivery, Services and Deployment at NAVBLUE (now Skywise), as Senior Product Consultant for Lido Navigation at Lufthansa Systems, and as a Business Development Manager for FlightAware — where he expanded global presence across the globe for each company.

Vincent holds an MBA from SDA Bocconi School of Management, during which time he also completed coursework at The Wharton School. He also holds a BBA with dual majors in International Business and Finance from The George Washington University.

A licensed private pilot with an instrument rating, Vincent brings a practitioner's perspective to the technology shaping how aircraft are planned, dispatched, and flown — equally comfortable in the boardroom negotiating a deal and in the cockpit experiencing firsthand what these tools are built to support.
